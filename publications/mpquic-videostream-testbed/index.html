MPQUIC Video Streaming Testbed

Performance Analysis of Multipath QUIC Schedulers for Video Streaming over Hybrid 5G-Satcom Networks

Authors:

Pattiwar Shravan Kumar , BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, India

Paresh Saxena , BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, India

Ozgu Alay, University of Oslo, Norway and Karlstad University, Sweden

Abstract:

This is a brief summary of the research paper. It provides an overview of the problem, methodology, results, and conclusions. With the rise of 5G/B5G, data-heavy vehicular IoT (V-IoT) applications demand smarter multipath scheduling. We propose two DRL-based MPQUIC schedulers, DEAR-MAC and DEAR-CAP, designed for dynamic vehicular networks. Tested with real-world traces and fluctuating network conditions, our methods outperform Peekaboo by up to 48.11%. These schedulers ensure more reliable and efficient data delivery for V-IoT use cases like real-time navigation and remote diagnostics...... Read Full Paper
